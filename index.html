The Solution

AEROS: The
Airport Brain

The Autonomous Eco-Responsive Airport Operating System (AEROS) unifies the airport's core operational systems — physical, logistical and environmental — into a single, coordinated architecture. Effectively eliminating delays, in turn reducing carbon emissions.

05 / 16
Core Technology Pillars

Three Operational
Pillars

01

Predictive Execution

"Predict → Optimize → Execute" cycle. The system eliminates congestion and wasteful idling before they happen.

02

Swarm Intelligence

A decentralized network of autonomous electric vehicles. Ensures mission continuity and adapts to ground traffic without a central failure point.

03

Carbon Reduction

Real-time calculation of carbon costs for every decision. AEROS will always make the decision with the least carbon cost without sacrificing speed or increasing cost.
